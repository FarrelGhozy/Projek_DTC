About Us

Bengkel DTC is a student-run technology service center at UNIDA Gontor, dedicated to providing friendly, transparent, and caring services. Our mission is to ensure that every student feels comfortable and supported in their technology needs.

D-Darussalam

Represents the identity of UNIDA Gontor as a pesantren and campus, upholding honesty, manners, and responsibility, with sincerity and devotion in every service.

T-Teknologi

Symbolizes technical expertise, innovation, and modern solutions in handling laptops, smartphones, printers, damage diagnosis, and basic digital device education.

C-Care

Service that goes beyond technical aspects — friendly, transparent, and supportive, ensuring comfort for all students.

Our Vision

“DTC represents the student-technologist: skilled in work, sincere in service.”
